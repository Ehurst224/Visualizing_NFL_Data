NFL statistics

On this site, you can navigate to three different analysis pages that will include statistics for players, teams, and positions. On the player analysis page you will be able to compare 2 player statistics of your choosing. On the team analysis page, you can select a specific team to view individual teammates fantasy points. On the bottom of the team page, you can find a chart including all team's accumulated fantasy points. The position analysis page will allow you to select a position to see each players fantasy point. A full set of statistics can be found on the data tab.
